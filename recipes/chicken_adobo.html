Chicken Adobo

Description:

A Filipino chicken dish covered in a soy sauce and vinegar based sauce. Typically marinated beforehand to deepen the flavors. Served on a bed of rice.

Recipes

  • Chicken thighs or breast
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken thighs in adobo sauce for at least an hour (more preferrably)
  2. Brown the chicken thighs on a hot pan and focus on the sear and the outside crisp
  3. Pour in the marinade and simmer to reduce the sauce
  4. Serve on a bed of rice and green toppings
