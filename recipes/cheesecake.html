Cheesecake

Description

A popular sweet dessert made with cream cheese with graham cracker crust as a base

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Sour Cream
  • Eggs
  • Graham Cracker Crust

Steps

  1. Create the graham cracker crust base
  2. Mix all the ingredients to create the cheesecake mix
  3. Pour in the mix to the graham cracker base
  4. Bake the cheesecake and let cool in the oven
  5. Serve!!!
